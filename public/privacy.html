Privacy
Policy.

PinMazon Cloud is a generate-only tool. An OpenAI API key entered into the form is transmitted over HTTPS to the PinMazon Vercel function, used for that generation request, and not written by the application to storage, application logs, cookies, localStorage, or the public source repository. The key field is cleared after each request.

Uploaded product images, product information and generated files are processed temporarily and discarded when the serverless request ends. Product information may be sent to OpenAI to generate copy. Do not submit personal, confidential or sensitive information.

PinMazon does not scrape Amazon pages and does not publish to Pinterest from the cloud version. Questions may be submitted through the public issue tracker; never include API keys or access tokens.
