Privacy
Policy.

PinMazon Cloud is a generate-only tool. In OpenAI API mode, a key entered into the form is transmitted over HTTPS to the PinMazon Vercel function, used for that request, and not written by the application to storage, cookies, localStorage, or the public source repository. The key field is cleared after each request.

In ChatGPT Plus manual mode, PinMazon does not send product information to OpenAI. You copy a prompt, use ChatGPT separately under its own terms, then paste returned JSON into PinMazon for local-style rendering.

Uploaded product images, product information and generated files are processed temporarily and discarded when the serverless request ends. Do not submit personal, confidential or sensitive information.

PinMazon does not scrape Amazon pages and does not publish to Pinterest from the cloud version. Questions may be submitted through the public issue tracker; never include API keys or access tokens.
