Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Overview

PinMazon is an internal application operated by Aesthetic Finds Lab. It helps the owner of an authenticated Pinterest business account prepare and publish reviewed content through the official Pinterest API.

Data we access

With the account owner's authorization, PinMazon may access the account's board list and the information required to create a Pin, including its title, description, image, destination link, alternative text, and selected board. The application is not designed to access unrelated Pinterest users' private data.

How data is used

Pinterest data is used only to display boards owned by the authenticated account, prevent accidental duplicate publishing, and publish content explicitly approved by the account owner. We do not sell, rent, or use Pinterest data for independent advertising profiles.

Storage and security

OAuth credentials and operational history are stored locally on the authorized operator's computer. Secrets and access tokens are excluded from the public source repository. Data is not intentionally transferred to Aesthetic Finds Lab servers.

Third-party services

PinMazon uses Pinterest API services and may use OpenAI API services to generate copy from product information supplied by the operator. Product information sent for generation must not contain personal or sensitive data.

Retention and deletion

The operator controls locally stored tokens, history, and generated files and may delete them at any time. Pinterest access can be revoked from the Pinterest account or by deleting the locally stored OAuth token. Published Pins can be managed in the authenticated Pinterest account.

Contact

Privacy questions can be submitted through the PinMazon issue tracker. Do not include passwords, API keys, access tokens, or other sensitive information.
