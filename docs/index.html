Aesthetic Finds Lab

Product content, prepared for Pinterest.

PinMazon is an internal content workflow that helps our team turn verified product information and original product images into reviewed Pinterest Pins.

What the application does

  • Creates Pinterest-ready 1000 × 1500 visual assets and metadata.
  • Lists boards belonging to the authenticated business account.
  • Publishes a Pin only after an account owner reviews and approves it.
  • Uses the official Pinterest API instead of automated browser actions.

Responsible data use

PinMazon is intended for Aesthetic Finds Lab's own Pinterest business account. It does not sell Pinterest data, access unrelated users' data, or scrape Amazon product pages. See our Privacy Policy for details.
